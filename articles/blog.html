My Blog Has Been Retired

Why?

I’ve finally retired the content from my old blog. It’s been years since I added anything to it and the annual renewal costs for the kehlet.cx domain are getting to be too much.

Was it all just so much garbage, to be thrown out?

No, I’ve preserved Subnetting is Fun (as well as its associated Subnetting Quiz) and Tuning TCP for High Bandwidth-Delay Networks, two articles I felt were genuinely useful. They can be found here under stevekehlet.com, which is much less expensive and I plan to keep around for the foreseeable future.

I really want to read about your “Water Heater Disaster” and see photos from your delicious “Chicken Dinner” article, can I still find them?

Yes, the Internet Archive Wayback Machine never forgets, you can find those articles and other gems archived there.
