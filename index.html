Tim Lum is a designer & entrepreneur at Pixel Flex.

At an early age I was fortunate to discover my passion for art & design. Exploring my creativity through many different mediums, I focused & honed my artistic strengths to the services I provide my clients today.

I attended Canadore College of Applied Arts, graduating with a Diploma in Graphic Design Productions and a Certificate in Interactive Multimedia with academic honours. During my education I started my freelance business by participating in the Ministry of Economic Development & Trade Program, Summer Company.

After graduation I worked as a designer at Penney & Company and Axiom Audio where I learned valuable fundamentals in the industry. I gained a wealth of experience in print design, photography, and ecommerce before launching Pixel Flex on October 1st 2008.

During my start-up phase I participated in the Canadian Youth Business Foundation (CYBF), which supported my financing and mentoring by Jesse Bennett-Chamberlain, 31Three.

I am currently a Registered Graphic Designer at Pixel Flex with over 8 years of work experience and always interested in new projects anywhere around the world.
